VLA

Over View

Vysya Limelight Awards is the brainchild of Siva Kumar Emmadi aka Emmadi, a Digital entrepreneur and Emmadi is also the founder of Vysya Business Network - VBN and an active member of the Vysya community. The genesis of the idea lies in the thought that all other communities were fusing their energies for the development of their own communities whereas the Vysya community was doing its own bit, but albeit in a limited way. Emmadi felt that the Vysya community also needs to come together more effectively and efficiently.

While more than 150-200 Vysya organisations exist and each one of them is working hard towards their own set objectives and goals there was not a single Association or Club that was formally celebrating the achievements of Vysya's in the country nor were they formally recognising their professional successes. Most of these entities were focusing on social and cultural spaces and very few were focussing on emerging technologies and businesses and how Vysya's can leverage them. Emmadi felt that changing dynamics need a changing thought process and he conceptualised Vysya Limelight Inc to be the vehicle to meet this goal.

Vysya Limelight Inc (VLI)has been set up to recognise and reward Vysya achievers and will focus on Awards/ Seminars/ Conferences/ Lectures/ Publishing etc and take up such activities that encourage the spirit of entrepreneurship and spirit of professionalism among members of the Vysya community. VLI will be run on professional lines and it is being actively supported by fellow Vysya professionals.
