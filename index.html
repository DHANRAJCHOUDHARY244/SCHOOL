Vysya Limelight Awards (VLA) is the brainchild of Siva Kumar Emmadi aka Emmadi, a Digital entrepreneur. Emmadi is also the Founder of Vysya Business Network (VBN) besides being an active member of the Vysya community. The genesis of the idea lies in the thought that all other communities were fusing their energies for the development of their own communities whereas the Vysya community was doing its own bit, albeit in a limited way. Emmadi felt that the Vysya community also needed to come together more effectively and efficiently.

While more than 150-200 Vysya organisations exist and each one of them is working hard towards their own set objectives and goals there was not a single Association or Club that was formally celebrating the achievements of Vysyas in the country nor were they formally recognising their professional successes. Most of these entities were focusing on social and cultural spaces and very few were focussing on emerging technologies and businesses and how Vysyas can leverage them. Emmadi felt that changing dynamics need a changing thought process and he conceptualised Vysya Limelight Inc VLI a corporate entity to be the vehicle to meet this goal.Vysya Limelight Awards for Men - 2023 (VLA4M-2023)Vysya Limelight Awards for Men - 2023 (VLA4M-2023)

Awards known as a mark of recognition given in honour of an achievement. Is also an encouragement to others to aim higher. It's a token to show one how far they have come and what they have gone through. Siva Kumar Emmadi

I Siva Kumar Emmadi extremely delighted to welcome you all to this first ever Vysya Limelight Awards. Permit me to say here that the Awards were the outcome of my long study of the Vysya business and professional community and the search for the best ways and means to empower the members of the community. During this long process I discovered a niche that the community was not formally celebrating the achievements and success stories of its members and there existed a need for honouring such people.
